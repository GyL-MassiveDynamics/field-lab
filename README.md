# Field Lab

Executable explorations of tensorial fields where separability fails, coherence emerges, and structure unfolds across regimes.

---

## Structural Context

This repository provides a computational environment for the exploration of tensorial configurations that cannot be reduced to separable forms.

The objects studied here are not introduced as isolated constructs, but as elements of a regime where structure is not imposed, but revealed through interaction, constraint, and transformation.

---

## Scope

The laboratory operates across multiple structural layers, including:

- Minimal tensorial regimes  
- Structural inseparability (rank ≥ 2)  
- Emergent coherence  
- Non-factorizable configurations  
- Dynamic structural evolution  

These layers are not presented as a sequence, but as aspects of a single unfolding structure.

---

## Method

Each concept introduced in the associated theoretical frameworks is accompanied by executable implementations.

The goal is not demonstration, but interaction.

Not explanation, but observation.  
Not assertion, but structural inevitability.

---

## Repository Structure

This laboratory is organized around formal structural objects as they become mathematically legible.

- `frameworks/` — foundational and emergent structural constructions  
- `core/` — reusable mathematical implementations  
- `notebooks/` — executable explorations and structural experiments  
- `protocols/` — operational rules for construction, documentation, and reproducibility  

The repository does not assume its final architecture in advance.  
Its structure unfolds only when new objects become formally unavoidable.

---

## Computational Philosophy

All structures in this repository are:

- Reproducible  
- Observable  
- Executable  

No object is introduced without the possibility of direct interaction.
